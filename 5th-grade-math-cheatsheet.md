## 📊 PEMDAS (Order of Operations)

**Remember: Please Excuse My Dear Aunt Sally**
- **P**arentheses ( )
- **E**xponents (powers, roots)
- **M**ultiplication & **D**ivision (left to right)
- **A**ddition & **S**ubtraction (left to right)

### 10 Examples:
1. 3 + 5 × 2 = **13** (multiply first: 3 + 10)
2. (8 - 3) × 4 = **20** (parentheses first: 5 × 4)
3. 6 + (2 × 3) = **12** (parentheses: 6 + 6)
4. 2 × (3 + 4) = **14** (parentheses: 2 × 7)
5. 9 - 4 + 2 = **7** (left to right: 5 + 2)
6. 5 + 2 × (3 + 1) = **13** (parentheses, then multiply: 5 + 8)
7. (6 + 2) × (3 - 1) = **16** (both parentheses: 8 × 2)
8. 7 + 3 × 2 = **13** (multiply first: 7 + 6)
9. (5 + 5) ÷ 2 = **5** (parentheses: 10 ÷ 2)
10. 8 - 3 × 2 + 1 = **3** (multiply: 8 - 6 + 1 = 3)

- Hold up 10 fingers
- To multiply 9 × 7: Fold down the 7th finger
- Count fingers: 6 on left, 3 on right = **63**

## 🔢 Divisibility Rules

- **Multiplication**: Multiply straight across

## 📐 Geometry (with Visual Aids)

### Square
```
  s
┌───┐
│   │ s    Area = s²
└───┘      Perimeter = 4s
```

### Rectangle
```
    w
┌─────┐
│     │ h   Area = l × w
└─────┘     Perimeter = 2(l + w)
```

### Triangle
```
    /\
   /  \  h
  /____\
     b      Area = ½ × b × h
            Perimeter = a + b + c
```

### Circle
```
     ___
   /     \
  |   •r  |    Area = πr²
   \ ___ /     Circumference = 2πr
```

### Cube
```
    ┌───┐
   /│   │\
  / │   │ \  s  Volume = s³
 └───────┘      Surface Area = 6s²
```

### 10 Examples:
1. **Square**: side = 4, Area = 4² = **16**
2. **Rectangle**: l=5, w=3, Area = 5×3 = **15**
3. **Triangle**: b=4, h=3, Area = ½×4×3 = **6**
4. **Circle**: r=3, Circumference = 2π(3) ≈ **18.84**
5. **Cube**: s=3, Volume = 3³ = **27**
6. **Sphere**: r=3, Surface Area = 4π(3²) ≈ **113.04**
7. **Trapezoid**: b1=4, b2=6, h=5, Area = ½(4+6)×5 = **25**
8. **Pythagorean**: a=3, b=4, c=√(3²+4²) = **5**
9. **Cylinder**: r=3, h=5, Volume = π(3²)×5 ≈ **141.37**
10. **Parallelogram**: b=5, h=3, Area = 5×3 = **15**

## ⚡ Exponents & Powers

### Rules:
- x² = x × x (x squared)
- x³ = x × x × x (x cubed)
- x⁰ = 1 (anything to power 0 = 1)
- x¹ = x (anything to power 1 = itself)

### 10 Examples:
1. 2² = 2 × 2 = **4**
2. 3² = 3 × 3 = **9**
3. 4² = 4 × 4 = **16**
4. 5² = 5 × 5 = **25**
5. 2³ = 2 × 2 × 2 = **8**
6. 3³ = 3 × 3 × 3 = **27**
7. 10² = 10 × 10 = **100**
8. 10³ = 10 × 10 × 10 = **1,000**
9. 1² = **1**
10. 0² = **0**

## 📝 Word Problems Strategy

### Step-by-Step Approach:
1. **Read** the problem carefully (2x!)
2. **Identify** what's being asked
3. **Underline** key numbers and words
4. **Choose** the operation (+, -, ×, ÷)
5. **Solve** step by step
6. **Check** your answer (Does it make sense?)

### Keywords:
- **Addition**: total, sum, altogether, combined, plus
- **Subtraction**: difference, less, remaining, left, minus
- **Multiplication**: times, product, each, per, groups of
- **Division**: split, share, per, divide, quotient

### 10 Examples:
1. **Train travels 60 miles/hour for 3 hours** → 60 × 3 = **180 miles**
2. **Cake cut into 8 pieces, 3 eaten** → 8 - 3 = **5 pieces left**
3. **10 apples, give away 4** → 10 - 4 = **6 apples**
4. **Book costs $10, buy 3** → 10 × 3 = **$30**
5. **Car holds 15 gallons, has 10** → 15 - 10 = **5 gallons needed**
6. **6 friends share 24 cookies** → 24 ÷ 6 = **4 cookies each**
7. **Read 2 books/week for 4 weeks** → 2 × 4 = **8 books**
8. **Dozen eggs = $3, buy 4 dozen** → 3 × 4 = **$12**
9. **Pencil = $0.50, have $5** → 5 ÷ 0.50 = **10 pencils**
10. **Run 5 miles/day for 7 days** → 5 × 7 = **35 miles**
